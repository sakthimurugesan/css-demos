background:linear-gradient( to bottom left,red,blue)

linear gradient percentage control

for example if we have specified linear-gradient(to bottom ,red 30%,green)

it means that from 0 to 30% red red should be there then smudging will be occur

smudging occurs for half of the remaining space for example i had mentioned red till 30% remaining 70% is there

the half of 70% is 35%

so from from above 30% to 65% smudging occurs

background:linear-gradient( direction,color start end)

we can also control smudging area between two colors

background:linear-gradient( direction,red 40%,green 60%)

here red will smudge after 40% and green will smudge after 60%

but there is no color after green so green will be completely there

from 40 to 60 smudging occurs
